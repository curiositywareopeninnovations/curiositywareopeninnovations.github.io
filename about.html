Our Mission

The Curiosityware Open Innovations is dedicated to fostering open-source innovation by providing cutting-edge software and hardware projects. Our goal is to empower developers and creators worldwide to leverage our projects, enabling them to release innovative software and hardware products with greater ease and efficiency.

To ensure maximum flexibility and accessibility, all our software projects are licensed under the MIT License, while our hardware projects are governed by the CERN Open Hardware Licence v2 - Permissive (CERN-OHL-P-2.0). These licenses grant complete freedom to use, modify, and distribute our work, encouraging widespread adoption and further development.

By choosing permissive licenses, we aim to remove barriers and provide developers with the tools they need to innovate freely, while ensuring proper attribution to the original creators. The Curiosityware Open Innovations is committed to democratizing technology and inspiring a new wave of open-source collaboration.
