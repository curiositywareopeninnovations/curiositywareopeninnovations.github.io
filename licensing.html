Licensing in Curiosityware Open Innovations

The Curiosityware Open Innovations is committed to fostering open-source innovation, aiming to empower developers and creators worldwide to utilize our projects as widely as possible. In this spirit, every project published on our platform is released under our own open-source licenses, ensuring both freedom of use and proper attribution.

Software Projects: COI Open Software License

All software projects released under Curiosityware Open Innovations are governed by the COI Open Software License. This custom license ensures that:

  • Freedom of Use: Anyone can freely use, copy, modify, and distribute the software for any purpose, including commercial applications, under the terms of the COI Open Software License.
  • Proper Attribution: The license requires that the original creator and source (Curiosityware Open Innovations) be clearly attributed in all derivative works and distributions.
  • Respect for Rights: Contributions must not violate personal property rights or cause any legal issues.

Copyright Information Under the COI Open Software License:

Copyright (c) 2025 Dominik István Bálló, Curiosityware Open Innovations

This guarantees that the project's origin and creators receive due recognition, while developers enjoy freedom in utilizing our software according to the COI Open Software License.

Hardware Projects: COI Open Hardware License

Hardware projects within Curiosityware Open Innovations, including all HDL (Hardware Description Language) based designs, are protected under the COI Open Hardware License. This license is specifically optimized for open hardware design and provides the following:

  • Maximum Flexibility: Designs can be freely used, copied, modified, and distributed, including for commercial manufacturing and sale, under the terms of the COI Open Hardware License.
  • Derivative Works: Users are permitted to develop their own products or solutions based on our designs, provided they comply with the COI Open Hardware License requirements.
  • Fair Attribution: The license requires that the original creator and source (Curiosityware Open Innovations) be clearly attributed in all derivative works and distributions.

Copyright Information Under the COI Open Hardware License:

Copyright (c) 2025 Dominik István Bálló, Curiosityware Open Innovations

This license ensures that our hardware innovations can spread widely and inspire further development, while the original creator and Curiosityware Open Innovations receive appropriate acknowledgment.

Our Shared Goal: Innovation and Recognition

Our choice of the COI Open Software License and COI Open Hardware License reflects our commitment to enabling Curiosityware Open Innovations projects to foster technological advancement, learning, and commercial innovation in the freest possible manner. Simultaneously, this approach ensures that Dominik István Bálló, as the founder, and Curiosityware Open Innovations itself, receive the recognition and visibility they deserve.

Contributor License Agreement (CLA) Required!

Before contributing to any Curiosityware Open Innovations project, you must first accept our Contributor License Agreement (CLA). This is essential to ensure that all contributions are legally sound and do not infringe on personal property rights or cause any legal issues.

  • The CLA protects both contributors and the project by confirming that you have the rights to your contributions.
  • It helps prevent the inclusion of content that could violate copyright, intellectual property, or other legal rights.
  • Only contributors who have accepted the CLA are permitted to submit code, documentation, or other materials to our projects.

Please review and accept the CLA before making any contributions!
